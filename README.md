# Physics-Simulations
Interactive physics simulations and visualizations:

-  Projectile Motion - Simple projectile motion parabola, with sliders for initial velocity and launch angle

-  Projectile Motion Improved - Projectile Motion Simulator, with time, live parabola tracking trail, and adjustable velocity and launch angle.

-  Simple Pendulum - Simple pendulum simulation . Click and drag the pendulum to set its starting position, then watch it swing and trace its path. Includes sliders to adjust the rod length and bob position (center of mass).
